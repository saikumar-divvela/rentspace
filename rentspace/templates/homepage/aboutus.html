About us

Started In 2016, Rahilo is an online platform where in you can get a secured temporary accommodation within your budget for few days. Rahilo helps you stay in a new place with local experience, similar to how you stay in your own place, with comfort.

Find A place across your town that feels like your own home. A flat for a night, a PG for a day or a room for 2 days, Rahilo is here for you to get you a right place with out a whole in your pocket. Finding friends in strangers, Rahilo helps you make network with new people.
